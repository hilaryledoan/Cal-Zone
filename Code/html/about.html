Who Are We?

The Hot Spot for pizza and beer. Home of downtown Berkeley's sensational pizza pies, gluten-free, vegan pizza, and appetizers. Using our signature three cheese blend and house-made pizza dough, we create a bold new pizza that dares to venture where no pizza has gone before. We also offer vegetarian and vegan options to meet any of your saucy desires.
