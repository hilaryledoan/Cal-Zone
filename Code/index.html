Vegan or Gluten-Free

We make our GLUTEN FREE pizzas with crusts from Venice Bakery. Each crust is made with rice flour, tapioca flour, water, potato starch, potato flakes, olive oil, sugar, yeast, and salt. Gluten-free pizzas are available in Large, Medium, Small, and Personal. Gluten-free pasta is now available.

Our vegan pizzas are made with Daiya mozzarella style shreds. Any size or style pizza can be made vegan or you can create your own.
